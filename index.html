Understanding Betting in Teen Patti


Teen Patti is a gambling card game that involves betting, bluffing, and strategic decision-making. Before diving into the game, it is important to understand the basics of how betting works.


  • Ante and Boot Amount – Each player contributes a fixed amount (boot amount) before the game starts.

  • Blind and Seen Play – Players can play blind (without seeing their cards) or seen (after checking their cards). Blind players bet less but take a higher risk.

  • Chaal and Side Show – Seen players can increase bets (chaal) or compare cards with another seen player (side show).
